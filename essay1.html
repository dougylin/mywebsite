Chips, US, TSMC, Culture

This essay is inspired by Intel's new process roadmap of 5 nodes in 4 years by 2025. It made me think about can and should American's do leading edge wafer fab manufacturing?

Yes America can, but it will require another Sputnik moment. Semi's are a long term, continuous investment. A one time $53B CHIPS Act is not enough when you consider The Economics of TSMC's Giga-Fabs. American interest in reshoring semi manufacturing will require a lot of training. The German and Taiwanese apprenticeship system is a model for how the next generation of semi process engineers should be grown at home.
